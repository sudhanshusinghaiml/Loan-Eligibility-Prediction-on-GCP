Loan Eligibility Check is the process in which Bank understand customers propensity to pay back the loan amount at a rate which a banks demands from them. Data Science has become a significant part of solving real-world problems. By leveraging business value with the help of Continues integration of data, and Continues deployment of models also known as CI-CD, it assists the continuous operation ofdata science models in production. In this project, We aim to deploy a model which predicts the likelihood of a customer to payback its full loan amount in the near future or not. We will use Google Cloud Platform (GCP) to build, train and deploy the model to predict the outcome.
